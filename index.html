Featured Projects

Knee Movement Simulator

   

In a group setting, I designed and developed a knee movement simulator using an Arduino Uno and a Servomotor. The Servomotor's task was to move within specific degrees, simulating the range of motion of a human knee.

Indoors Localization

   

As a part of a team, I developed Python code that performs indoor localization of a Raspberry Pi with the help of 3 BLE Beacons. After the platform's location is determined, it is visualized along with the beacons' locations.

P.A.M.O.C

 

For a group project, me and my team created the prototype for a device that detects and calms panic attacks, called P.A.M.O.C. If the system determines that there is an attack, it provides relaxing activities to the user.

Gaia

During a Hackathon, our team developed Gaia. An app that aims to reward restaurants when they adopt behaviors to reduce their food waste. The ancient Greek theme gives the users the opportunity to create their path to Olympus while minimizing their environmental footprint.
